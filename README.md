# Privacy Policy for 5min Facts

Last updated: May 14, 2026

5min Facts is a free educational mobile app designed to help users learn short lessons across topics such as History, Psychology, Money, Health, and Stoicism.

## Information We Collect

5min Facts does not require users to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or login information.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

- Lesson progress
- Saved insights
- Streak progress
- App settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our own servers.

## Advertising

5min Facts uses Google AdMob to show ads.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide, measure, and improve advertising. This may include:

- Device identifiers
- Advertising ID
- App interactions
- Approximate location
- Diagnostic information
- Ad performance information

For more information about how Google uses data, visit:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including:

- Google AdMob
- Google Play services

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

## Children’s Privacy

5min Facts is not directed specifically at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Storage

Learning progress and saved insights are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, this local data may be deleted.
